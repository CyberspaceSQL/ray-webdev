Taco Trivia

When did Tacos first appear in Nigeria?

Tacos are not traditionally Nigerian food, but they likely made their first appearances in Nigeria through international restaurants, expatriate communities, and fast-food chains. While it's difficult to pinpoint an exact date, Mexican cuisine, including tacos, gained popularity in Nigeria in 2010, especially in major cities like Lagos and Abuja. (Source: NaijamexFoodie Lagos)
